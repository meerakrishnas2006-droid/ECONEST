🌱 Technology

Our Features

Six integrated systems working together to create a self-sustaining home ecosystem.

💧

Hydroponics

Plants are grown without soil using nutrient-rich water, allowing efficient food production in small spaces.

🌿

Vertical Farming

Crops are grown in vertical layers to maximize food production while occupying minimal floor space.

🥕

Square Foot Gardening

A compact gardening method that organizes crops into small square sections for efficient space utilization.

🌧️

Rainwater Harvesting

Rainwater collected from the terrace is stored and reused for irrigation, reducing freshwater consumption.

♻️

Composting

Kitchen and garden waste are converted into organic compost, providing natural fertilizer for plants.

🌞

Solar Energy

Solar panels generate clean electricity to power pumps, lights, and other smart farming systems.

About The Project

This project demonstrates how a modern home can practice sustainable agriculture in limited space using hydroponics, vertical farming, square foot gardening, rainwater harvesting, composting, and solar energy. A simple smart dashboard allows users to monitor and control these systems, promoting efficient resource management and environmentally friendly living.
